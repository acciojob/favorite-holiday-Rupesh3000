Paris, France


Paris, the capital of France, is known as the "City of Light". It is a world-famous destination for art, culture, history, and exquisite cuisine. From iconic landmarks to romantic streets, Paris offers an unforgettable experience.

Top 5 Attractions in Paris

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic symbol of Paris and a must-see attraction.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the world-famous painting Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of French Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – Famous for its theaters, cafés, and luxury shops.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – A stunning basilica with panoramic views of the city.

Must-Try Foods in Paris

  • Croissant – A buttery, flaky pastry that is a breakfast staple.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes that can be sweet or savory.
  • Baguette – A classic French bread with a crispy crust.
  • Macarons – Colorful and delicate French cookies filled with ganache.
  • Ratatouille – A traditional vegetable dish packed with flavor.
